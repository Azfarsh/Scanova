By leveraging **advanced AI technologies** and modern development frameworks, Scanova simplifies traditional healthcare processes and connects users with medical services regardless of location.

## ğŸš€ **Features and Benefits**

### **Core Features**
1. **Smart Screening Services**:
   - Skin Cancer Detection
   - Breast Cancer Detection
   - Lung Cancer Detection
   - Tuberculosis Screening
   - Parkinson's Disease Detection

2. **Voice-based Health Check-up**:
   - Provides health insights based on voice input.
   - Powered by IBM Watson AI and Speech-to-Text services.

3. **Personalized Recommendations**:
   - AI-driven chatbot delivers tailored healthcare guidance.

4. **Medical Document Management**:
   - Secure and organized storage for health records.

5. **Remote Healthcare Access**:
   - Connects users with doctors and specialists from different regions.

6. **Cost-Effective Solutions**:
   - Reduces the financial burden of traditional screenings.

7. **Advanced Tech Stack**:
   - **Frontend**: TypeScript + Vite Framework
   - **Backend**: Python
   - **Database and AI Services**: IBM DB2, Auto AI, Watson AI, LVM Model, and Speech-to-Text.
